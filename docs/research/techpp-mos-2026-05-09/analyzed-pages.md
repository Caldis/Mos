1. `https://techpp.com/2024/06/29/change-scrolling-directions-for-mac-mouse-and-trackpad-separately/`
   - Title: "How to Change Scrolling Directions for Mac Mouse and Trackpad Separately"
   - Author: Sagar Naresh
   - Updated: 2024-06-29
   - Mos evidence summary:
     - The article says macOS has no built-in separate scroll-direction setting for mouse and trackpad, and lists Mos as one third-party solution.
     - It recommends Mos when the user wants granular controls rather than only reversing direction.
     - It describes Mos as open source and lightweight.
     - It says Mos can reverse scrolling direction, set speed, step, and duration, and customize behavior per app.
     - It lists Mos pros as high customizability, per-app behavior, adjustable speed/duration, and free/open-source availability.
     - It lists Mos cons as requiring macOS 10.11 or later and having a more complex setup than alternatives.
     - The setup steps mention downloading Mos from the official website, installing the DMG, launching it, granting accessibility permissions, and enabling Reverse Scroll.
